Entities
********

At the heart of Blitz3D lies the concept of an 'Entity'.

An Entity is a 'thing' in the 3D world that has a position and an orientation.

Blitz3D supports many kinds of entities:

* Camera
* Light
* Pivot
* Mesh
* Sprite
* Plane
* Mirror
* Terrain
